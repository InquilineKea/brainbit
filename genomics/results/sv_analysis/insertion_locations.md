## Potential Functional Impact

Insertions in different genomic contexts can have varying functional impacts:

1. **Insertions within genes** may disrupt gene function, particularly if they occur within exons.
2. **Insertions near promoters** may affect gene expression by altering transcription factor binding or chromatin structure.
3. **Insertions near CpG islands** may impact epigenetic regulation of nearby genes.

The analysis shows that:

- 907 insertions (42.97%) are within genes, potentially affecting gene function
- 18 insertions (0.85%) are within exons, which could directly impact protein coding
- 121 insertions (5.73%) are near promoters, potentially affecting gene expression
- 54 insertions (2.56%) are near CpG islands, which could impact epigenetic regulation
