## Collagen-Related Conditions

### COL1-Related Conditions

Variants in COL1A1 and COL1A2 genes are associated with:

- **Osteogenesis Imperfecta**: A group of genetic disorders that mainly affect the bones, causing them to break easily
- **Ehlers-Danlos Syndrome (Classical Type)**: Affects connective tissue, leading to skin hyperextensibility, joint hypermobility, and tissue fragility
- **Caffey Disease**: An infantile disorder characterized by bone changes, soft tissue swelling, and irritability

### COL7-Related Conditions

Variants in COL7A1 gene are associated with:

- **Dystrophic Epidermolysis Bullosa**: A genetic condition that causes the skin to be fragile and blister easily
- **Epidermolysis Bullosa Acquisita**: An autoimmune disorder causing skin fragility and blistering
